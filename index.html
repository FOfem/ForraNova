    Resource Lab: Engineering & Analysis

    Status: High-Precision Mode Active

    1. The Quantum Graphing Calculator

    This is not a standard pocket calculator. It is a mathematical engine designed to solve and visualize complex data.

    Equation Scanning: Paste or type complex strings. The calculator uses math.js logic to solve derivatives, integrals, and algebraic functions instantly.
    • Graphing Engine: Every time you solve an equation, the system can plot a visual graph. This helps you see "trends" in data rather than just numbers.
    • Variable Memory: The calculator remembers your previous results, allowing you to use them in the next step of your research.
    • Handwritten Recognition: When linked with the Sketchpad, the AI attempts to "OCR" (recognize) handwritten numbers and move them into the solve-box.

    2. The Digital Probe & Workbench

    The "Probe" is the most powerful research tool in the Hub. It acts as a bridge between the outside world and your Neural Vault.

    How it works: The Probe "dissects" files and external apps to find the "ingredients" required for the ForraNova Academy.
    • File Projection: Click the "Project" icon to display a file on the main analytical screen. Here, you can highlight text, extract images, and tag important facts.
    • Ingredient Extraction: As you find key facts in a document, click "Harvest." These facts are sent to the NeuralDB, where the Academy will later use them to build your Exam Phases.
    • Deep Scanning: Use the Probe to scan images for text (OCR). If you have a photo of a textbook page, the Probe will turn it into editable text for your Reader.

    3. Neural Sketchpad (Free-hand)

    Integrated directly into the Lab, the Sketchpad is for visual thinkers.

    • Schematic Drafting: Perfect for drawing circuit diagrams, tailoring patterns, or architectural layouts.
    • Vector Export: Drawings are stored as high-quality data. You can save your sketches to the Digital Treasury to keep a visual record of your research.
    • Auto-Smoothing: The AI helps "clean up" your lines, turning shaky hand-drawings into professional-looking schematics.

    The Lab-to-Academy Pipeline

    For a "Dummy," the flow is simple:

    1. Calculate: Solve your technical problems in the Quantum Calculator.
    2. Probe: Analyze your study materials and "harvest" the facts.
    3. Sketch: Visualize your ideas on the Sketchpad.
    4. Transfer: Click "Sync to Academy" to move all this Lab data into your Exam Vault.

    Lab Tip: Use the "Deep Scan" feature on images to save hours of typing!

    Digital Trove: The Data Core

    Storage Engine: NeuralDB (IndexedDB)

    1. Your Infinite Filing Cabinet

    The Trove is where every "ingredient" harvested in the Lab and every score earned in the Academy is permanently filed.

    100% Local Privacy: Because this uses your browser's internal database, your files never touch a cloud server. Your research is yours alone.
    • Neural Records: Every exam you finish creates a "Record" here.
    • Ingredient Storage: Text snippets harvested from the Digital Probe are stored here for the AI to use later.
    • System Persistence: This room ensures that when you close your browser and return tomorrow, your level, mastery points, and themes are exactly where you left them.

    2. Sorting the Vault

    As you use the Hub, the Trove gets crowded. We have built-in "Neural Filters" to help you find what you need.

    Filter Type Function
    Subject Filter Isolates data for a specific field (e.g., only "Mathematics").
    Chronological Sorts your research from newest to oldest.

    The search bar in the Trove is linked to your Student Desk. Typing a single keyword will scan thousands of data points instantly.
